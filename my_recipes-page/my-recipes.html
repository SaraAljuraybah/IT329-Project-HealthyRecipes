Mediterranean Quinoa Box
Ingredients
  • Cooked quinoa - 150g
  • Cherry tomatoes - 60g
  • Cucumber - 50g
  • Feta cheese - 40g
  • Kalamata olives - 30g
  • Lemon vinaigrette - 2 tbsp
Instructions
  1. Cook quinoa according to package directions and let cool
  2. Dice cucumber and halve cherry tomatoes
  3. Crumble feta cheese into small pieces
  4. Layer quinoa in lunchbox compartment
  5. Arrange vegetables, olives, and feta on top
  6. Pack dressing separately to add before eating

Turkey & Veggie Wraps
Ingredients
  • Whole wheat tortillas - 2 pieces
  • Sliced turkey breast - 120g
  • Lettuce leaves - 4 pieces
  • Sliced bell peppers - 60g
  • Hummus - 3 tbsp
  • Baby carrots - 80g
Instructions
  1. Lay tortillas flat on clean surface
  2. Spread hummus evenly on each tortilla
  3. Layer turkey slices in the center
  4. Add lettuce and bell peppers on top
  5. Roll tightly and cut in half diagonally
  6. Pack wraps and carrot sticks in separate lunchbox sections

Protein Power Box
Ingredients
  • Hard-boiled eggs - 2 pieces
  • Grilled chicken strips - 100g
  • Mixed nuts - 40g
  • Cheese cubes - 50g
  • Grape tomatoes - 60g
  • Whole grain crackers - 8 pieces
Instructions
  1. Boil eggs for 10 minutes, then cool and peel
  2. Grill chicken breast and slice into strips
  3. Cut cheese into bite-sized cubes
  4. Arrange eggs in first lunchbox compartment
  5. Add chicken strips to second compartment
  6. Fill remaining sections with nuts, cheese, tomatoes, and crackers

Rainbow Veggie Bento
Ingredients
  • Edamame - 80g
  • Shredded carrots - 60g
  • Purple cabbage - 50g
  • Snap peas - 70g
  • Brown rice - 120g
  • Sesame ginger dressing - 2 tbsp
Instructions
  1. Cook brown rice and let it cool slightly
  2. Steam edamame and snap peas for 3-4 minutes
  3. Shred carrots and thinly slice purple cabbage
  4. Pack rice in largest lunchbox section
  5. Arrange colorful vegetables in separate compartments
  6. Store dressing in small container to drizzle before eating
