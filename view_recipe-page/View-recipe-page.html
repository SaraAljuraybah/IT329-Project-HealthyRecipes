Instructions

  1. Peel the boiled eggs and place them in a small bowl.
  2. Add the Greek yogurt, salt, and pepper, then mash everything with a fork until creamy.
  3. Toast the whole-grain bread slices until they are golden and crispy.
  4. Spread the mashed avocado on one side of the toast.
  5. Top with the egg mixture and sprinkle some chili flakes for an extra kick.
  6. Close the sandwich, cut it in half, and enjoy your healthy meal!
