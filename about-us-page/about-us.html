About Lunchy

Lunchy is a web application designed to help you discover, create, and share healthy lunchbox recipes. Whether you're meal prepping for the week or looking for quick lunch ideas, we've got you covered.

Our platform makes it easy to browse recipes, save your favorites, and organize your meal plans all in one place. Join our community and start your journey to healthier eating today!

This project was created as part of a web development course to demonstrate modern web design and user experience principles.
